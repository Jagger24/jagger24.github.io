Capstone Design: Software Applications (5911)

Description

Capstone design project: application of software engineering techniques, methodologies and technologies in software lifecycle activities using enterprise software frameworks; teamwork, written and oral communication. Prereq: 3231 or 5231, and 2501 or Philos 1338, and CSE 3901 or 3902 or 3903, and second writing course; or Grad standing. Units: 4 credit hours.
